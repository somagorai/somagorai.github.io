  • UX/UI Design

    What started as an ardent interest in the art of making posters and visual communication soon turned into getting into the psyche of people - what do people see when they interact with an interface? What are their grievances? How do we bridge the gap between what needs to be communicated and what is communicated? And so I have been trying to understand people, and craft experiences for them, for the past 4 years :)

  • Data Analytics

    My interest in Data came with Design. What proves that design is good? Why should we favour one design over another? How does one justify any decision, really? I have come to find data to be a very strong weapon, albeit something which has to be used very, very carefully. Balancing the right amount of data and intuition is my favourite adventure sport ;)

  • Product Management

    I should not call Product Management a skill (lol) since it is a never-ending list of qualities impossible to be present in a single human being. But I strive to be to learning, iterating and gracefully exiting wherever required. I am obsessed with Root Cause Analysis - and Service and System Design. Knowing the entire life cycle of a product and every user journey inside out is essential for a sustainable solution.

R-Store for Channeli

Ideated and designed R-Store WebApp for Channeli users (official IITR Portal that IMG maintains), which lets them showcase and receive feedback and discuss their created apps on a single platform; received an average of 200 users per day in the first week with 80% positive feedback

Rethinking WhatsApp Business

I did this case study as an assignment for Razorpay’s Design Challenge in January 2022. The problem statement was to design an experience to enable the local stores to sell items to their customers through WhatsApp for Businesses.

Patronus for Creatica 2021

Patronus was a project I did for Creatica 2021 hackathon. Patronus is an application which suggests the safest route based on existing police database. It is a safety app which can also be used to notify your close contacts in case of any mishaps.
