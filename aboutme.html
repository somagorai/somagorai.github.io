My name is Soma Gorai and I am a 3rd year Architecture UG at IIT Roorkee. I am currently based in Delhi NCR, India, for the past two years, since the onset of the lockdown.
Architecture being a design field got me interested in the way products are designed - from the problems they aim to solve to the process of solving the problems, and finally putting the solution to test.
More than Architecture, Product Design is a very iterative process, in the sense that it is viable to iterate a product after it has been launched (so we have MVPs and High Performance). This gives the designers a certain freedom to take risks and experiment, which is what interested me the most.
I luckily got selected as a designer for our institute's website maintaining student group, Information Management Group. There I learnt most of the workings of UI/UX design from my projects and seniors and peers. I enjoy creating art - be it **painting, sketching or graphic art**. I mostly use Adobe Illustrator.
Apart from these, I love reading books. I have read over 150 books, mostly fiction. My favorite book is The Book Thief by Markus Zusak and I really like reading Murakami. I also have been reading psychology and design books recently and found the Sapiens especially enlightening.
I also play Age of Empires 2 a lot and am an ancient and medieval history buff.
